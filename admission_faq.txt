Q1: What is the eligibility for B.Tech?
A1: Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects and obtained at least 60% aggregate marks.

Q2: Which entrance exams are accepted for B.Tech admission?
A2: The university accepts JEE Main, state-level entrance exams, and its own entrance test for B.Tech admissions.

Q3: Is there a management quota for admission?
A3: Yes, a limited number of seats are available under the management quota based on merit and interview performance.
